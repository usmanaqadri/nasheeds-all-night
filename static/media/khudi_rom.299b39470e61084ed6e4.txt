Khudī Kā Sirr-e-Nihāṅ
Khudī kā sirr-e-nihāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
Khudī hai taygh fasāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
Yeh daur apnē Brāhīm kī talāsh meiṅ hai
Sanam-kada hai jahāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
Yeh māl o daulat-e-dunya yeh rishta o paivand
Butān-e-vahm o gumāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
Kiyā hai tū nē matāʿ-e-ghurūr kā saudā
Farayb-e-sūd o ziyāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
Agarche but haiṅ jamāʿat kī āstīnōṅ meiṅ
Mujhe hai hukm-e-azāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
Yeh naghma fasl-e-gul o lāla kā nahīṅ pāband
Bahār hō ke khizāṅ lā ilāha illā l-lāh
