Rōk Lētī Hai
Rōk lētī hai āp kī nisbat
Tīr jitnē bhī hum pe chaltē haiṅ
Yeh karam hai huzūr kā hum par
Ānē vālē azāb ṭaltē haiṅ
Ab kōʾī kyā hamēṅ girāʾē gā
Har sahārā galē lagāʾē gā
Hum nē khud kō girā liyā hai vahāṅ
Girnē vālē jahāṅ sambhaltē haiṅ
Apnī auqāt sirf itnī hai
Kuchh nahīṅ bāt sirf itnī hai
Kal bhī ṭukṛōṅ pe unkē paltē thē
Ab bhī ṭukṛōṅ pe unkē paltē haiṅ
Vohī bhartē haiṅ jhōliyāṅ sab kī
Voh samajtē haiṅ bōliyāṅ sab kī
Āʾō bāzār-e-Mustafā kō chalē
Khōṭē sikkē vahīṅ pe chaltē haiṅ
Zikr-e-sarkār kē ujālōṅ kī
Be-nihāyat haiṅ rifʿatēṅ Khalid
Yeh ujālē kabhī na simṭēṅgē
Yeh voh sūraj nahīṅ jō ḍhaltē haiṅ
