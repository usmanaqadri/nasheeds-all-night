Jashne Amad e Rasul
Jasne Amade Rasool, Allah Hi Allah
Bibi Amna Ke Phool, Allah Hi Allah
Jab Kay Sarkaar Tashrief Lane Lage,
Huro Gilma Bhi Khushiyan Manane Lage
Har Taraf Noor Ki Roshni Chaa gayi,
Mustafa Kya Milay Zindagi Mil gayi,
Aie Halima Teri Goadh Mein Aa Gaye,
Dono Alam Ke Rasool, Allah Hi Allah
Chehra-e-Mustafa Jab Dikhaya Gaya
Chhup Gaye Tarey Aur Chand Sharma Gaye
Aminah Dekh Ker Muskuranay Lagein,
Hawa, Mariam Bhi Khushiyaan Mananay Lagein,
Amina Bibi Sab Say Yeh Kehnay Lagein,
Dua Ho Gayi Qubool, Allah Hi Allah.
